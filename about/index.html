About

Optimum Mag

Optimum Mag is an English-language photography magazine built around cameras, lenses, field craft, imaging workflow, and the working habits of photographers.

Our Focus

We publish practical, image-led reviews and guides: camera tests, lens comparisons, technique tutorials, gear workflow notes, and interviews that help readers make sharper creative decisions.

Why Optimum Mag

Optimum shares its root with optics, while Mag points to both magazine and magnification. The name reflects our editorial habit: look closely, compare clearly, and explain what matters.

How It Is Published

Articles are written in Markdown, managed like an Obsidian vault, and published through a fast Next.js site with localized AVIF imagery.
