<p>Wotancraft&#039;s Full Leather Portable Camera Pouches bring the brand&#039;s signature vintage-meets-functional aesthetic to a compact form factor. Available in four sizes and crafted from Italian vegetable-tanned cowhide, these pouches protect compact cameras and accessories while looking good enough to carry solo.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Size Guide</h2>
<p>| Size | Fits | Weight | |------|------|--------| | S (0.54L) | Ricoh GRIII, Fujifilm X Half, Sony RX100 | 110g | | M (1.12L) | Fujifilm X100VI, Leica D-Lux 8, DJI Mini 3 Pro | 170g | | ML (1.66L) | Leica Q, Sony A7C, Nikon Z fc | 170g | | L (1.94L) | Fujifilm X-M5, Sony A6600, A7 IV (w/ compact lens) | 210g |</p>
<hr />
<h2>Build &amp; Materials</h2>
<p>YKK zippers glide smoothly, paracord attachment loops accept shoulder or wrist straps, and every pouch includes a soft microfibre interior lining compatible with Wotancraft&#039;s Velcro modules. An external zipper pocket on all sizes holds memory cards, cables, or keys. The leather starts stiff but develops a beautiful patina with use.</p>
<p>Wotancraft also offers matching Parashooter Straps in neck and wrist lengths — available with Peak Design Anchor Links or brass CURA split-rings. The straps use Atwood 550 paracord (rated to 250kg) with leather finishing that matches the pouches perfectly.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Real-World Versatility</h2>
<p>These pouches work as standalone grab-and-go bags, drop-in organizers inside larger backpacks, or protective cases for drones and accessories. The L size&#039;s included divider lets you split the interior for a body plus spare lens. For those who prefer canvas, Wotancraft offers identical pouches in water-repellent military-grade 500D canvas at roughly half the leather price.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Practical Tips</h2>
<ul>
<li>Size up if you want to toss in spare batteries or a cleaning cloth alongside your camera.</li>
<li>The M size doubles as an excellent protective case for a DJI Mini Pro drone.</li>
<li>Pair with the Parashooter neck strap with Peak Design Anchor Links for quick swapping between setups.</li>
</ul>
<hr />
<h2>FAQ</h2>
<p><strong>Q: Do I need Wotancraft&#039;s straps or can I use my own?</strong> Any strap works — simply attach to the paracord loops. The Parashooter straps match aesthetically.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How does leather compare to the canvas version?</strong> Leather feels more premium, develops patina, and has natural water resistance. Canvas is tougher against scrapes, fully weatherproof, and more affordable.</p>
