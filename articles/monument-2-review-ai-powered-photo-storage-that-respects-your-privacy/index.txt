<p>The Monument 2 is a physical photo storage device with built-in AI that backs up, organizes, and secures all your photos — without monthly fees or cloud-server dependency. All processing happens locally on the device, which means your data stays private.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Key Specs</h2>
<ul>
<li>Quad-core ARM CPU + 2 TOPS NPU (AI accelerator)</li>
<li>Built-in SSD: 1TB or 2TB (replaceable M.2 SATA)</li>
<li>Diskless version also available (uses external USB)</li>
<li>Wi-Fi 6, Gigabit Ethernet, Bluetooth 4.0</li>
<li>Ports: USB 3.0 ×2, SD card slot, HDMI (4K TV out)</li>
<li>256-bit AES + SSL/TLS encryption, password protection</li>
<li>Face, pet, and scene recognition — all processed locally</li>
</ul>
<hr />
<h2>How It Works</h2>
<p>The Monument 2 connects to your home network and syncs photos from phones, tablets, computers, and cameras automatically. Its AI scans every image, identifies faces and pets, eliminates duplicates, and organizes everything into searchable categories — all without sending your data to external servers.</p>
<p>The device is small (5.2&quot; square, 1.4&quot; tall), durable enough to travel, and always-on. Transfer speeds are fast thanks to SSD storage. The companion app supports iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, and Linux.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Monument 2 vs Monument 1</h2>
<p>The second generation is approximately 10× faster than the original, adds built-in SSD storage (the V1 required external drives), introduces pet detection and password protection, and upgrades connectivity with USB 3.0, dual-band Wi-Fi, and 4K TV output.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Price</h2>
<ul>
<li>Diskless: $199</li>
<li>1TB SSD: $399</li>
<li>2TB SSD: $499</li>
<li>Optional cloud backup: $49.99/year (4TB)</li>
</ul>
<p>One-time purchase, no mandatory subscriptions. The SSD is user-replaceable when full.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Practical Tips</h2>
<ul>
<li>Set up automatic phone backup immediately — it runs in the background and you&#039;ll never worry about losing photos again.</li>
<li>Use the password protection feature for sensitive albums.</li>
<li>The SD card slot lets you dump camera cards directly without a computer.</li>
</ul>
<hr />
<h2>FAQ</h2>
<p><strong>Q: Does Monument 2 replace Google Photos or iCloud?</strong> Yes — it offers similar AI organization features but keeps everything local, with no recurring fees.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How many photos can 2TB hold?</strong> Hundreds of thousands of JPEGs, or tens of thousands of RAW files — sufficient for most photographers&#039; entire libraries.</p>
