<p>After weeks testing the DJI Mavic 4 Pro across Colorado&#039;s mountains and the desert southwest, I can confirm this is a meaningful upgrade over the Mavic 3 Pro — sharper images, longer battery life, and the best controller DJI has ever made.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Key Specs</h2>
<ul>
<li>3 cameras: 100MP Wide (4/3&quot; CMOS, f/2), 48MP Medium Tele (70mm, f/2.8), 50MP Tele (168mm, f/2.8)</li>
<li>6K 60p / 4K 120p video</li>
<li>52-min flight time per battery (Creator Combo: 3 batteries)</li>
<li>512GB internal SSD</li>
<li>Omnidirectional obstacle avoidance with LiDAR</li>
<li>RC Pro 2 controller with 7&quot; 2000-nit touchscreen</li>
<li>Weight: 2.3 lbs (1063g)</li>
</ul>
<hr />
<h2>Real-World Performance</h2>
<p>The Mavic 4 Pro is larger and heavier than the 3 Pro, but the extra heft translates to stability in the air. The RC Pro 2 controller is a dramatic upgrade: pop-out sticks, folding antennas, bright 2000-nit screen, and near-instant mode switching. I could get three flights per battery versus two on the Mavic 3.</p>
<p>Battery charging is now parallel — charge three simultaneously instead of sequentially. The 512GB internal storage eliminates microSD anxiety. USB-C direct transfer from the back of the drone is simple and fast.</p>
<p>Image quality is exceptional: 100MP stills and 6K 60fps video show noticeably more detail than the Mavic 3 Pro&#039;s 5.1K 50fps. The f/2 main lens gathers a full stop more light for cleaner low-light footage. Vertical video mode — just rotate the controller screen — is a game-changer for social content.</p>
<hr />
<h2>Practical Tips</h2>
<ul>
<li>Hand launching takes practice due to the larger size — start in open areas.</li>
<li>The Creator Combo is worth it for pros: extra batteries and parallel charging save hours on multi-day shoots.</li>
<li>Obstacle avoidance is excellent even through tree lines, but stay cautious in low light.</li>
</ul>
<hr />
<h2>FAQ</h2>
<p><strong>Q: Does the Mavic 4 Pro shoot vertical video natively?</strong> Yes — rotating the controller screen rotates the gimbal for 6K vertical capture.</p>
<p><strong>Q: How big are the 100MP files?</strong> Very large. Budget for significant storage. The 512GB internal SSD helps enormously.</p>
